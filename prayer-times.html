The Importance of Salah

Prayer is the second pillar of Islam and was the first act of worship made obligatory. The Prophet Muhammad (peace be upon him) said: "The first matter that the slave will be brought to account for on the Day of Judgment is the prayer."

Prayer is a direct connection between the worshipper and Allah - no intermediaries needed.

٥

The Five Daily Prayers

  • Fajr - 2 units - Before sunrise
  • Dhuhr - 4 units - After midday
  • Asr - 4 units - Late afternoon
  • Maghrib - 3 units - After sunset
  • Isha - 4 units - Night prayer

New to Prayer?

Learning to pray is a beautiful journey

Basic Steps of Prayer

  1. Make Wudu (Ablution) - Ritual washing before prayer
  2. Face the Qibla - Direction of the Kaaba
  3. Make Intention (Niyyah) - In your heart
  4. Say "Allahu Akbar" - Begin the prayer
  5. Recite Surah Al-Fatiha - The opening chapter
  6. Perform Ruku (Bowing) - Bow with hands on knees
  7. Perform Sujud (Prostration) - Place forehead on ground
  8. Complete the prayer - With Tashahhud and Salam

Don't worry about perfection - start slowly and learn gradually. Allah values your sincere effort.
