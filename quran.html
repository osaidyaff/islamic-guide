About the Holy Quran

The final revelation from Allah to mankind

قرآن

What is the Quran?

The Quran is the literal word of Allah, revealed to Prophet Muhammad (peace be upon him) through Angel Jibreel (Gabriel) over 23 years. It contains 114 chapters (Surahs) and over 6,000 verses (Ayahs).

محفوظ

Perfectly Preserved

The Quran is the only religious scripture that has been preserved in its original language, letter by letter, for over 1,400 years. Millions of Muslims have memorized it entirely.

هدى

Guidance for All

The Quran provides guidance on all aspects of life: worship, family, business, justice, and spirituality. It is a mercy and healing for the hearts of believers.
